About

I think like a designer,
execute like an engineer,
and move between both worlds
without losing the details.

I've worked in multi-camera live production since 2010, across television, esports, corporate events, game shows, awards ceremonies, and large-scale branded events. IMAG, live streaming, or both.

Since 2024 I've designed and taught "Building Community Through Live Streaming," a graduate course at the University of Washington that helps professionals understand what live streaming can do that pre-recorded content can't, and why it matters. The course is where my experience in production meets my interest in brand strategy and community design, and where I get to develop those ideas with a new generation of communications professionals.

I work best with teams who respect the craft while not being afraid to take creative risks, experiment with new workflows, and set out to build something no one has done before.

Over the years I've won an Emmy award and multiple Tellys.
